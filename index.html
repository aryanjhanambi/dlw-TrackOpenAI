WELCOME TO MERIDIAN

Meridian is a human-governed AI delivery platform. Teams define policy guardrails, create governed tasks, run approvals with mandatory human checkpoints, and keep an auditable institutional memory of decisions.

  • onboarding stays shared across all projects
  • each project keeps its own tasks, approvals, memory, and audit trail
  • switch projects from the top-right selector anytime
